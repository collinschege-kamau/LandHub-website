Selling Land Simplified

Three simple steps to turn your property into profit.

How It Works

For Buyers

1

Discover & Map

Browse through a wide variety of land listings. Use ourInteractive Leaflet Maps to see exactly where a property is located before you even leave your house.

2

Take a virtual tour

Get a real feel for the terrain. Watch mandatory high-definition property videos to inspect the land’s boundaries and surroundings.

3

Stay Notified

Can't find what you're looking for? Save your search criteria, and we will send you an instant notification the moment land in your preferred area hits the market.

4

Connect Directly

No middlemen. Access the seller’s verified contact details and phone number to start negotiations immediately.

For Sellers

1

Create an account

Complete your profile as a buyer (you will be directed to view posted land) OR as a seller (you will be directed to sellers dashboard to post your first land).

2

Create your listings

Use our intuitive Seller Dashboardto list your property in minutes. Give your land a title that stands out and set your price.

3

Pin your location

Use our integrated map tool to drop a pin on your property. This gives buyers confidence in the exact location and neighborhood.

4

Show, Don't just tell

Upload a mandatory video tour of your property. Listings with videos receive significantly higher engagement and "likes" from serious buyers.

5

Track intrest

Monitor your dashboard to see how many people have "favorited" your land. Once you've closed the deal, simply mark it as "SOLD" with one click.
